Who am I?

Hey! My name is Ethan and I am an aspiring graphic designer and photographer with a love for anything related to content creation. I actually didn’t draw much as a child so I always felt like my artistic skills were lacking in that realm but once I discovered graphic design and the idea that anything you think of can be created with the computer on my desk, my whole mindset changed and I fell in love with learning all of the software that is related to my work.

I have always had a fondness for brand design but in the recent years, I have started to really enjoy animation as well as UI/UX design. I really hope my dedication to the craft shows through in my designs and I will continue learning as much as I can to ensure I provide the best art possible.

What is depth?

Depth was created because I found myself constantly searching for new content related to graphic design on websites such as YouTube but would have a less than enjoyable experience when some of the same topics were being taught in a classroom setting. I realized that formal teaching is not my preferred method of learning about a new subject.

This prompted me to research the true value of informal versus formal learning and I discovered that it is very common for people, especially younger generations, to have a more difficult time being taught formally and I wanted to do my part to help another person who may learn similarly. This Youtube Shorts video series serves to provide design fundamentals to newer graphic artists who have a much easier time learning informally.
